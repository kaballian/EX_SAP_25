\section{Dependency Analysis}


\subsection{Purpose}
It is imperative that we're aware who our service providers are, this is to prevent
certain types of attacks, such as those aimed at our supply chain. If we don't commit do out due 
diligence in vetting the providers, we are make ourselves vulnerable, which could undermine
the efforts to ensure the safety of the company.
